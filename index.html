Beaufort West Homestead Project

Your DIY guide to building a sustainable homestead step-by-step

Project Overview

This homestead project is structured into 4 main phases:

  • Phase 1: Essentials & minimal livable setup (14 days) - R40,200
  • Phase 2: Infrastructure expansion and operational scaling - R45,000
  • Phase 3: Operational expansion and system optimization - R40,000
  • Phase 4: Full self-sufficiency and long-term infrastructure (2-5 years) - R125,000
  • Property Purchase: R556,000 (R520,000 + R36,000 transfer)
  • Total Investment: R806,200 (with R750,000 financing available)

Property Specs & Project Goals

  • Location: 8 km from Beaufort West, Western Cape, South Africa
  • Property Size: 21 hectares (52 acres)
  • Purchase Price: R520,000 (excluding transfer costs)
  • Existing Infrastructure: Borehole (partially equipped), two 5,000L water tanks, shipping container
  • Key Goals: Safety, water security, temporary shelter, basic food production, phased self-sufficiency
  • Phase 1 Focus: Essential livable setup within 14 days using canvas tents and existing container
  • Timeline: Full self-sufficiency in 2-5 years through systematic development
